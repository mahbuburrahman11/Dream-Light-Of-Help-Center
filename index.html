Welcome To Our Website

Dream Light Of Help Center is a Youth and service center.This organization is fully non profitable and non political.The main objective of this organization is to help the needy people.We have helped the helpless people for a long time.We created many non profitable events in last 3 years.

Our Story

Our organization initiative on 20th September 2019 and our journey begins on 1st November 2019. Since its inception, our organization has created many charity events. Helped students to gain their knowledge through various quizzes. We are very happy that we have already been able to put a smile on many people's faces

Our Mission

Our mission is to help the poor and needy people. Our main aim is to feed the poor and help the needy people. We also help students to acquire basic knowledge through quizzes. We want people of our country to live a happy and peaceful life.

Our Dream

Our dream is that no people in our country should starve. No man should be in the trouble of necessary things. Students should get their proper basic knowledge. And all the people of the country should lead a healthy life

Our Recent Activity

Recently we have created many events and helped many needy people. We have collaborated with many organizations to help more people in the future. Here is a list of some of our events.

Distribute Foods in Sylhet

You may know about the terrible flood in Sylhet. We are happy to announce that we have been able to help around 300 families with their basic necessities from our event.

Quiz Competition

Due to covid 19 students could not go to school. As a result they are almost away from their education. So we organized quiz event for students according to syllabus so that they can cover their studies to some extent.

Collaboration With South Bangla Hospital

We organize free blood test events. So we co-operate with South Bangla Hospital so that we can easily get the blood test supplies easily.

Free Food During Ramadan

The month of Ramadan is very blessed so we have organized free food to make proper use of this blessed month. And we are very happy we have completed the event peacefully

Collaboration With Italy Broadband Network

Recently we have collaborated with Italy Broadband. They helped us a lot. They sponsored us T-shirts. They have promised that they will help us in the future as well.

Winter Cloths Distribution

In winter, the life of people without winter clothes becomes very difficult. So we provide free winter clothes to people who don't have winter clothes and we have successfully Distributed winter cloths to needy people.
